Our Story

Redefining the art of brewing, One cup at a time!
BREWED is all about creating comfort, great conversations, and providing magical moments for our guests.

Our voyage from the bean to the container began in year 2015.Our first cafe in 1996 at Brigade Road in Bangalore – the adolescent and the youthful on a fundamental level quickly took to the cafe, and it keeps on being a standout amongst the most happening spots in the city.

Find yourself relaxed and having fun while 'working from home' as you sip one of our unforgettable brews


We believe that self-discovery happens through experience, whether it be traveling the world in search of the finest coffees, splitting a mini-donut with your little one or meeting for your first coffee with “the one”. That’s why our mission is to engage people with an immensely satisfying experience. If you need a place to work, we have a well-lit specific seating area, with high-speed internet connection especially for that.

SERVING OUR SIGNATURE ALL DAY FOOD AND DRINKS MENUS, ALONG WITH OUR FRESHLY ROASTED COFFEE, OUR STYLE IS OPEN, RELAXED AND FRIENDLY.

Here at BREWED, we believe: Engaging with others over a meal or tasty beverage is good for the soul. Collaborating with our neighbors is better than competing. Everyone has value and deserves a second chance!We envision sustainable growth as the most satisfying coffee brand in Bangalore. With a focus on unique and well-crafted food and drinks, outstanding customer engagement and a relentless pursuit of excellence and delight, Brewed will be recognized as the go-to in Bangalore for an immensely satisfying experience.
